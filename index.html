About Me

Hello, I'm Gustiyan Islahuzaman from Purbalingga, Central Java, Indonesia. I hold a Bachelor's degree in Statistics from Diponegoro University with a GPA of 3.50.

I have hands-on experience as a Data Analyst Intern at Psyliq, where I analyzed employee data using Excel and MySQL, and developed an interactive HR dashboard. At Cognifyz Technologies, I specialized in creating Power BI solutions, focusing on data cleaning, transformation, and visualization. During my time at Technohack Edutech, I worked with complex data sets, utilizing RStudio and Python for data preprocessing and developing dashboards with Power BI and Tableau.

I am proficient in R, Python, SQL, and advanced Excel (Power Query and Power Pivot). My skill set includes tools like RStudio, VSCode, DBeaver, Google Sheets, Google Colab, Power BI, and Looker Studio. I am passionate about transforming complex data into insightful, interactive visualizations to inform smarter decision-making processes.
