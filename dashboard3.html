SALES DASHBOARD FOR DATA-DRIVEN INSIGHTS

Introduction

Within the scope of my training journey, Iâ€™ve developed a comprehensive Sales Dashboard using Google Looker Studio, harnessing data from the Vrinda Store Data on Kaggle. The dashboard consists of three pages: Home, Geomap, and Data, offering a complete view of our sales data.

Data Source

The Sales Dashboard is powered by a robust dataset containing 21 columns and 31,047 rows of sales data. This extensive dataset serves as the foundation for in-depth analysis and insightful visualizations.

Challenge

Our sales data was scattered across various systems and lacked cohesion, making it challenging to identify trends and opportunities.

Solution

I integrated Looker Studio with our data sources, enabling a seamless flow of information. With its intuitive interface, I created a customizable dashboard that consolidated all relevant sales data.

Key Features

  • Sales Revenue Trend: A dynamic chart showcasing our monthly sales revenue trends.
  • Sales by Channel: An interactive visualization of sales distribution across different channels.
  • Top State: A chart displaying our best-performing states.
  • Demographics of Customers: Insights into customer demographics for targeted marketing.
  • Customer Detail Table: A comprehensive table providing individual customer profiles, including ID, age, gender, and sales spending.
  • Bubble Map for State: A map visualization providing a comprehensive view of sales data by state.

Conclusion

The Sales Dashboard not only streamlined our sales processes but also became a potent tool to remain competitive in a swiftly changing market. It serves as a testament to the transformative potential of data visualization when paired with the right tools and insights.
